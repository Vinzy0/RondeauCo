## SIGNATURE COCKTAILS

**The Rondeau Old Fashioned** — 19
George Dickel Bottled-in-Bond bourbon, Demerara syrup, Angostura and orange bitters, expressed orange peel, smoked with cherry wood tableside. Our take on Nashville's most-ordered cocktail — slow, deliberate, and built to last.

**Charcoal Negroni** — 18
Ford's gin, Campari, Carpano Antica Formula sweet vermouth, stirred with activated charcoal for a velvety finish. Served over a single hand-cut ice sphere, garnished with a flamed orange twist.

**Smoke & Honey** — 17
Vida mezcal, wildflower honey syrup, fresh lime, muddled serrano pepper, Tajín rim. Bright, herbaceous heat balanced by Tennessee wildflower sweetness.

**The Cellar Door** — 20
Rémy Martin 1738 cognac, St-Germain elderflower, fresh lemon, champagne float, edible lavender. An elegant, aromatic pour inspired by the quiet drama of our underground Cellar dining room.

**Cumberland Mule** — 16
Chattanooga Whiskey 91-proof rye, house ginger-lime cordial, Fever-Tree ginger beer, fresh mint, Angostura dash. Crisp, spicy, and dangerously easy to drink.
