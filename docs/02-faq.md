### RESERVATIONS

**How do I make a reservation?**
Reservations can be made through our website at rondeauandco.com, via the Resy app, or by calling us directly at (615) 555-0187. We recommend booking at least 7–10 days in advance for weekend seating, and 2–3 weeks in advance for parties of six or more.

**How far in advance can I reserve?**
Reservations open 30 days in advance on a rolling basis.

**Do you accept walk-ins?**
We do our best to accommodate walk-in guests, but availability is limited. We reserve a small number of seats at the bar and lounge for walk-ins each evening on a first-come, first-served basis.

**What is your group dining policy?**
For parties of 6–10 guests, we offer reserved seating in our main dining room. For groups of 10 or more, we recommend our private dining rooms. Please contact our events team at events@rondeauandco.com.

---

### CANCELLATION POLICY

**What is your cancellation policy?**
We kindly request that cancellations or changes be made at least 24 hours prior to your reservation time. Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows will incur a $50 per person fee charged to the card on file.

**What about the tasting menu?**
For guests who have pre-booked our seasonal tasting menu, cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. A $100 per person fee applies for late cancellations or no-shows, as ingredients are sourced specifically for your reservation.

### DRESS CODE

**What should I wear?**
Rondeau & Co. maintains a smart casual to upscale dress code. We ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, flip-flops, tank tops, or excessively casual attire. Jackets are welcomed but not required. Think refined Southern — you're here for an occasion.

---

### PARKING & TRANSPORTATION

**Is there parking available?**
Complimentary valet parking is available every evening at our 3rd Avenue entrance. Self-parking is available at the SoBro Garage on 4th Avenue South, a two-minute walk from the restaurant. We validate for up to three hours.

**Do you recommend rideshare?**
Absolutely. Our host team is happy to assist with Uber or Lyft requests. Our drop-off point is clearly marked at the main entrance.

---

### DIETARY ACCOMMODATIONS

**Can you accommodate food allergies or dietary restrictions?**
Yes. Our kitchen team takes allergies and dietary needs seriously. We accommodate gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, vegetarian, and vegan requests with advance notice. Please note any restrictions when making your reservation, and confirm with your server upon arrival.

**Do you have a separate vegetarian or vegan menu?**
While we don't publish a separate menu, our chefs can prepare a full multi-course vegetarian or vegan experience with 48 hours' notice. Several dishes on our standard dinner menu are already vegetarian or can be easily modified.

**Are your menus labeled for dietary needs?**
Yes. Our dinner menu includes (V) for Vegetarian, (GF) for Gluten-Free, and (DF) for Dairy-Free labels on all applicable dishes. If you have a specific allergy not covered by these labels, please speak with your server.

### PRIVATE DINING & EVENTS

**Do you offer private dining?**
Yes. We have two private dining spaces — The Hearth Room (up to 18 guests) and The Cellar (up to 40 guests). Both are available for private events, corporate dinners, celebrations, and wine dinners.

**How do I book a private event?**
Please contact our events team at events@rondeauandco.com or call (615) 555-0189. We recommend booking at least 3–4 weeks in advance. Full details, including menus and pricing, are available in our Private Dining & Events guide.

### GIFT CARDS

**Do you offer gift cards?**
Yes. Physical gift cards are available at the restaurant, and digital gift cards can be purchased through our website. Gift cards do not expire and can be applied to food, beverage, and gratuity.

### CHILDREN & FAMILIES

**Is the restaurant kid-friendly?**
While we welcome guests of all ages, Rondeau & Co. is best suited for older children and teens who are comfortable in a fine dining setting. We do not offer a separate children's menu, but our kitchen is happy to prepare simplified dishes upon request. High chairs are available.
