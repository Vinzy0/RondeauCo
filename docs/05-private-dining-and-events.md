### AN EXPERIENCE, NOT JUST A RESERVATION

At Rondeau & Co., private dining is an extension of everything we do in our main dining room — only more personal. Whether you're hosting a corporate dinner, celebrating a milestone, or gathering friends for a curated wine evening, our team will craft an experience tailored entirely to your occasion.

Every private event includes a dedicated captain, a custom menu designed in collaboration with Chef Elias Marchetti, and a wine program built by Sommelier David Paik to complement each course.

### THE CELLAR

*Underground. Atmospheric. Dramatic.*

Located below the main dining room, The Cellar is a raw-brick, barrel-vaulted space originally built as a cold storage chamber in the 1920s. It has been restored and fitted with a custom 14-foot live-edge Tennessee walnut communal table, a dedicated bar, and a temperature-controlled wine wall displaying select bottles from our reserve collection.

### HOW TO BOOK

1. **Inquire.** Email events@rondeauandco.com or call (615) 555-0189 with your preferred date, guest count, and occasion. Our events team responds within one business day.

2. **Consult.** We'll schedule a call or in-person visit to walk through the space, discuss menu preferences, and align on your vision.

3. **Confirm.** A signed event agreement and a 25% deposit secure your date. The remaining balance is due 5 business days prior to your event.

4. **Finalize.** Final guest count and menu selections are confirmed one week before your event. We handle the rest.

---

### POLICIES

- A 22% service charge and applicable Tennessee sales tax (9.25%) are added to the final bill.
- Cancellations made within 14 days of the event will forfeit the deposit.
- Cancellations within 72 hours are subject to the full minimum spend.
- Outside food is not permitted. Outside dessert cakes are allowed with a $50 cutting and plating fee.
- Outside alcohol is not permitted unless pre-approved; a $65 per bottle corkage fee applies.
